ctrl+o to open scene file

ctrl+shift+s to save scene file as
space+leftclick to pan editor camera
space+rightclick to rotate editor camera
space+middleclick to zoom editor camera
scrollwheel to zoom editor camera
space+~ to reset editor camera
space+leftalt to toggle perspective vs orthographic camera for editor camera
Q to disable modifying with mouse in scene
W to enable moving entity position in scene gizmo
E to enable rotation gizmo
R to enable scale gizmo
cntrl c, x, and v for copy cut and paste
